Winter

Snow. Ice. Snowball fights. All of these can be seen during the coldest season of the year. Winter creates a white landscape that is perfect for skiing, snowboarding, and sledding. It also makes for some classic Christmas weather.








Make sure you have warm jackets and a good pair of boots, because WINTER IS COMING.
